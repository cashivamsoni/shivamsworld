About the Brand

Welcome to Shivam’s World - more than just a YouTube channel, it’s a space where creativity meets simplicity. Run by Shivam Soni, this channel celebrates the joy of making with ideas for arts and crafts that anyone can try. From elegant calligraphy and expressive drawings to DIY projects and handcrafted cards for every occasion, Shivam’s World brings together inspiration and technique in equal measure. About Shivam - Creativity Meets Discipline

Shivam Soni is a passionate creator and CA Intermediate student from Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh, who blends academic dedication with artistic flair. Having excelled in his studies - including topping his school in Class 12 with outstanding marks - Shivam continues to pursue professional excellence while nurturing his love for creativity. Beyond accounting and finance, he is known for his advanced calligraphy, handcrafted cards, and DIY projects, which he shares through his brand Shivam’s World. His journey reflects a balance of discipline and imagination: from building entire websites with thousands of lines of code, to designing vibrant stationery and exploring content creation on YouTube and Instagram. Shivam’s work is marked by attention to detail, innovation, and a genuine desire to inspire joy and creativity in others.
